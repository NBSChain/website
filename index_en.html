ABOUT

NBS is committed to encrypting and saving digital assets in the form of distributed storage,
registering the ownership of digital assets in the form of blockchain bookkeeping,
embodying different rights of use of different digital assets with differentiated token values and promoting
its rapid distribution and circulation. The encrypted storage of digital assets,
and the chain of storage records, through the decentralized distribution and circulation of value,
eliminates the excess profits obtained by the centralization organization because of its monopoly position,
and therefore provides technical support for digital asset creators to obtain fair and just returns
